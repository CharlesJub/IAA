## Basics
### Conditional Probability
The probability of event A given event B occurring. 
#### Bayes' rule:
$P (A | B) = \frac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)}$
$P(A)$: prior
$P(B|A)$: likelihood
$P(A|B)$: posterior
If $P(A|B) = P(A)$, then A and B are independent
